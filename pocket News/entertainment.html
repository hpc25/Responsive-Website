Lord of the Rings set to become the most expensive TV show of all time!

The small screen adaptation of Lord of the Rings is all set to be the most expensive TV series ever. Amazon has acquired the rights of the series, adapted from JRR Tolkien's books for $250 million, according to The Hollywood Reporter. The five-season series is expected to cost upward of $1 million, with all castings, producers, and visual effects factored in.

Salman Khan Granted Bail By Jodhpur Court!


Now in a great relief for Salman Khan, the Jodhpur Sessions Court has given bail to the actor in the blackbuck hunting case. The actor has to pay the bail bond of Rs 50,000. Earlier, the superstar was sentenced to 5 years of imprisonment and had been slapped with a fine of Rs 10,000 for poaching two endangered blackbucks in 1998.
