India's forex reserves at life-time high!


Foreign exchange reserves of India hit a life-time high of USD 424.361 billion after rising by USD 1.828 billion in the week to March 30, helped by rise in foreign currency assets, the Reserve Bank said today. In the previous week, the reserves had increased by USD 1.197 billion to USD 422.532 billion. Previously, the reserves had touched USD 421.914 billion on Feb 9.



Crypto exchange sold for $34 mn after suffering $530 mn hack


Japanese crypto exchange Coincheck, which lost about $530 million after suffering the world's largest cryptocurrency hack ever, was acquired by online brokerage firm Monex Group for about $34 million. Coincheck's management, including current CEO Koichiro Wada and COO Yusuke Otsuka, will step down as part of the deal. Monex shares jumped 20% after the announcement.



Patanjali to launch garments line in 2019


Stating that consumers want him to launch Patanjali jeans, yoga guru Baba Ramdev has announced that the brand will launch its own garments line next year. The collection, which will also feature ethnic clothes, will cater to men , women and children. He added that Patanjali will bring "windfall profits" to India from the countries which "looted" the nation.



Flipkart teams up with MakeMyTrip for online bookings


Flipkart today announced its tie-up with MakeMyTrip for travel bookings. Users can now make travel bookings through MakeMyTrip's services using Flipkart's mobile application. Partnership will be launched with domestic flight bookings in the next few weeks, followed by hotels, bus, and holiday bookings. Financial details of the partnership has not been revealed yet.
