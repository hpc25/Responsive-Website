Commonwealth Games 2018: Sathish Kumar Wins Weightlifting Gold


India won their third gold medal in as many days in weightlifting at the Commonwealth Games 2018 after Sathish Sivalingam came first in the men's 77kg category on Saturday. The 25-year-old lifted a total of 317kg to win his second successive gold medal at Commonwealth Games. All of India's five medals at CWG 2018 till now have come in weightlifting.



IPL 2018: Preview: CSK face MI


Returning after serving a two-year suspension, two-time winners Chennai Super Kings will take on reigning champions Mumbai Indians in the opening match of the Indian Premier League at the Wankhede stadium here on Saturday (April 7). Mumbai Indians, led by Rohit Sharma, will have their task cut out against Chennai Super Kings under Mahendra Singh Dhoni.



Meet the youngest Indian weightlifter to win a medal at CWG 2018


Teenager Deepak Lather today became the youngest Indian weightlifter to claim a Commonwealth Games medal, clinching a bronze in the men's 69kg category at Gold Coast. The 18-year-old from Haryana lifted a total of 295kg (136kg+159kg) to finish third after his nearest rival, Vaipava Loane, fouled his last two lifts to end with a total of 292kg.



Gareth Bale wants to stay at Real Madrid


Real Madrid's Gareth Bale, who is being linked with moves away from the club, is reportedly keen to stay at Real. Bale has had a topsy-turvy season with a mix of injuries and good performances. With 14 goals and 7 assists, the Welshman has struggled to become first choice for Zidane this season. Spanish journalist Guillem Balague says he will be sold in the summer.
