Lok Sabha wasted 127 hrs, functioned for 34 in Budget session


The Lok Sabha lost 127 hours and 45 minutes during the budget session due to frequent disruptions and forced interruptions, while the House functioned for around 34 hours during 29 sessions. An average of 0.5% starred questions were answered per session, with only 17 of the 580 starred questions being answered. Written replies were tabled for remaining questions.



Opposition to me turning violent: PM


Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Friday said their opposition to him is turning "increasingly violent" due to the fact that people born in backward castes are in the country's top positions and also because of their discomfort with the BJP's rising strength. His remarks came against the backdrop of violent protests during a 'Bharat bandh'.



Pakistani terrorist arrested after managing to escape earlier


A terrorist, who had recently managed to escape during an operation by security forces, was arrested last night by the Jammu and Kashmir Police. Zabiullah, a Pakistani, was arrested after a cordon and search operation in the Hyhama area of Juggiyal village in Kupwara. A member of Lashkar-e-Taiba, Zabiullah had escaped during an operation in Halmatpora on 20 March.
